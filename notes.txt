1. Install
> helm install orthweb ./orthanc --debug | less
2. Monitor till pods are up
> kubectl get po
3. Turn on port forwarding
> kubectl port-forward service/orthweb-postgresql-ha-pgpool 5432:5432
4. Fetch secret
> kubectl get secret orthweb-postgresql-ha-postgresql -o jsonpath="{.data.postgresql-password}" | base64 --decode
5. Validate
> psql --host=localhost --port=5432 --username=postgres
